How it started

A Vision to Demystify Science

Bwenke was founded in 2018 by a group of passionate university researchers and high-school science instructors who grew tired of standard, dry rote memorization techniques. They recognized a widening disconnect between abstract board formulas and real physical comprehension.

What started as a small, specialized weekend chemistry tutoring class in Nairobi has exploded into a state-of-the-art educational sanctuary. Today, Bwenke provides multi-track systems covering pure chemistry, biology, official CBC frameworks, and future-forward programming competencies.

Our Mission

To empower every student with hands-on critical thinking, practical science competencies, and digital literacy tools required to excel academically and lead the technological future.

Our Vision

To stand as the leading innovation-driven science and pre-technical academy in the region, bridging classroom instruction with experimental life-science applications.
