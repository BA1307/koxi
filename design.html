Visual & Tech Sciences

Design & Technology

Bridge the gap between technology, visual layout, and digital sciences. Our design program teaches digital interfaces (UI/UX), 3D structural modeling for molecules and organs, visual science communication, and technical design engineering.

Specialization Tracks Design Lab Tools

Computational Design

Interactive 3D wireframing, layout systems, and prototyping.

Technical Syllabus

Computational Design Focus Areas

A meticulous blending of software mastery, artistic aesthetics, and structural scientific diagrams.

01

UI/UX & Interactive Product Design

Master wireframing, high-fidelity layouts, design systems, modern typography structures, mobile and web responsive interfaces, and human-centered design heuristics using industry standards.

02

3D Structure & CAD Modeling

Learn Computer-Aided Design (CAD) workflows, sculpting 3D proteins, biological cells, chemical crystal structures, electronic circuit boards, and scientific device hardware modeling.

03

Visual Science Communication

How to design clear, publication-grade academic charts, schematic diagrams for biology and chemistry, informational scientific posters, and clean typography layouts.

04

Web Design & Creative Frontend

Explore the styling systems of the web including responsive CSS layouts, interactive grid cards, SVG animation vectors, canvas-based visualizations, and intuitive design-to-code principles.

Industry Standards

The Bwenke Digital Design Suite

We train our students on premium computational tools utilized by design agencies, biochemical laboratories, and research institutions globally. Our labs provide complete, guided licenses for standard suites.

✓ Figma: Vector prototyping and interface layouts
✓ Blender & AutoCAD: 3D meshes and parametric rendering
✓ Adobe Illustrator: Technical drawing, schematic layouts
✓ Tailwind CSS & SVGs: Creative web code components

Practical Capstone Project

Every student in the Design track graduates by presenting a functional digital prototype. This can range from an interactive educational chemistry app design, a customized 3D molecular model visualizer, or a complete biology science web interface.
